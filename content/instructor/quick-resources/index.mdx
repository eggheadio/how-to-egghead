### Desktop Environment
- Use large fonts and high-contrast color schemes
- Record fullscreen at **16:9 aspect ratio**
- **HiDPI** mode on monitor: **1280x720 pixel** resolution
    1. [How on Windows](screencasting/screen-setup#dpi-scaling-on-windows)
    2. [How on MacOS](screencasting/screen-setup#access-hidpi-mode-on-macos)
- Use **2:1 ratio** of **code:example**:

### Exporting Videos
- Export video files at **2560x1440 MPEG-4** at **30 FPS**
- Naming: **sequential number** and a **slug** (ie: *01_workshopname_lessonname.mp4*)
- [Export your video from Screenflow](screencasting/export-screenflow/)
